Allifmaal - A Suite of Business Management Applications

Allifmaal is a suite of integrated applications that are designed to work seamlessly together to streamline your business operations.

Allifmaal Modules

Allifmaal ERP comprises of the below sub-modules that work togther to achieve the goals of your organization.

CRM

The Customer Relation Management module is used to manage customer related activities like customer registrations, customer payments customer statements, customer account activities and much more.

HRM

The Human Resource Management module is used to manage staff related activities like employees registrations, staff payments and benefits, salaries, employee statements, employee account activities and much more.

FRM

The Financial Resources Management module is used to manage finance related activities like accounts, payments, debts, credits, financial statments and much more.

SRM

The Supplier Relation Management module is used to manage supplier related activities like supplier registrations, supplier payments supplier statements, procurement, orders, supplier account activities and much more.
